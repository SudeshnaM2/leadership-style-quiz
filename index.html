Leadership Style Quiz

This quiz is based on the North, South, East and West Leadership Compass. It is a simple framework used in leadership training to show that people lead in different ways, and that no single style is better than another.

The framework was developed as part of group and leadership development work to help people understand how they approach decisions, relationships, ideas and action. It is widely used in workshops to improve teamwork, communication and self awareness.

Each direction represents a different way of leading:

  • North: action and results
  • South: people and relationships
  • East: ideas and possibilities
  • West: logic and detail

Most people are a mix of styles. This quiz will help you understand your main style and how to work effectively with others.
