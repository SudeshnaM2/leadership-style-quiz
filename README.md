# Leadership Style Quiz

This is an interactive quiz based on the North, South, East and West Leadership Compass.

The tool helps users understand their leadership style and how they approach decision making, relationships, ideas and action.

## What the quiz does

- Identifies your primary leadership style
- Detects mixed styles where relevant
- Explains your strengths
- Highlights areas to watch out for
- Provides practical advice on how to perform well
- Gives guidance on how to work with other leadership styles

## The framework

The Leadership Compass is a simple model used in leadership development and group work. It shows that people tend to lean towards different approaches:

- North: action and results
- South: people and relationships
- East: ideas and possibilities
- West: logic and detail

Most people are a mix of these styles. The goal is not to label people, but to improve self awareness and teamwork.

## How it works

Users answer a set of scenario based questions. Each answer contributes to one of the four styles.

The quiz then:
- Calculates scores across all styles
- Identifies the strongest style or combination
- Displays a personalised result with guidance
